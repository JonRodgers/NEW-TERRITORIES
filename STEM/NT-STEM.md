# New Territories STEM Curriculum
## The World's Greatest STEM Course

A revolutionary curriculum inspired by **Atmospheres** that celebrates the intersection of **Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics (STEAM)** through the lens of architectural, engineering, and construction innovation in the UK and Hong Kong.

---

## 🎯 Our Philosophy

We believe STEM education should be:
- **Creative & Imaginative**: Projects that spark curiosity and wonder
- **Historically Grounded**: Understanding how pioneers shaped our world
- **Comparative**: Exploring the evolution from past to present innovations
- **Interdisciplinary**: Blending science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics
- **Practical**: Hands-on learning through real-world applications
- **Inspiring**: Celebrating the inventors and engineers who changed humanity

---

## 🏗️ Competitive Advantages

### UK & Hong Kong Context
- **Architectural Heritage**: From Victorian engineering to modern sustainable design
- **Construction Innovation**: Leading practices in urban development and infrastructure
- **Global Perspective**: Comparing British engineering traditions with Asian innovation
- **Real-World Projects**: Access to case studies from iconic structures and developments
- **Industry Connections**: Partnerships with architects, engineers, and construction professionals

---

## 📚 Core Curriculum Structure

### Age Groups & Progression
- **Primary (Ages 5-7)**: Foundation & Wonder
- **Primary (Ages 8-11)**: Exploration & Creation
- **Secondary (Ages 12-14)**: Innovation & Design
- **Secondary (Ages 15-18)**: Advanced Engineering & Entrepreneurship
