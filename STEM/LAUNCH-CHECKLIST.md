## 🎯 Success Metrics

Track these metrics to measure success:

- **Form Completion Rate**: % of started forms that are submitted
  - Target: 70%+

- **Booking Conversion Rate**: % of website visitors who book
  - Target: 5-10%

- **Email Response Time**: Average time to respond to inquiries
  - Target: Under 24 hours

- **Student Enrollment**: Number of students enrolled per month
  - Target: 3+ per class

- **Class Utilization**: % of classes that meet minimum 3 students
  - Target: 90%+

- **Parent Satisfaction**: Feedback from parents
  - Target: 4.5+ out of 5 stars
